# Privacy Policy for Khepri

**Last Updated:** April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Khepri mobile application ("the App") handles your data. Khepri is designed to help you take daily photos and create timelapses. We prioritize your privacy and ensure that your data remains explicitly under your control.

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** 
Khepri is a completely offline application. We do not have servers, and your data never leaves your device.

## 2. Permissions We Request

To provide its core functionality, Khepri requires the following device permissions:

*   **Camera (`android.permission.CAMERA`)**: Required to take photos within the app so you can build your daily timelapses.
*   **Storage (`android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, `android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`)**: Required to save the photos you take directly to your device's local storage and to retrieve them so the app can display them and align your next shot.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

All photos taken with the Camera and saved to your Storage remain solely on your device. We do not upload, share, sell, or analyze your photos. The data physically belongs to you and resides safely on your local hardware.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks that collect data from you.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app does not collect user contact information, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
