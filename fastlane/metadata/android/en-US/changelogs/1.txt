Initial release of Khepri — the timelapse camera app.

• Capture timelapse sequences with your camera
• Ghost overlay for precise frame alignment
• Manual frame alignment — pan, pinch-to-zoom, and rotate with a ghost reference overlay
• Precision alignment panel with numeric offset X/Y, rotation and zoom controls
• Review, reorder (drag & drop), and delete frames
• Tap a frame to open full-screen viewer; tap again to advance to the next frame
• In-app timelapse preview with adjustable speed
• Transition effects: Dissolve, Fade, Flip (page-turn) with configurable duration
• H.264/MP4 export and share
• Rename, clone, and organise multiple projects
